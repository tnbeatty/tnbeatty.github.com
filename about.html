a bit more about me

My name is Nate Beatty, but you already know that because you are currently visiting natebeatty.com. I am currently a physics major (and a math minor) at Middlebury College in Vermont. When I am not working on problem sets, I enjoy programming, and spending time outside. Middlebury is located in one of the most beautiful places on the planet, and I am lucky to have spent four years enjoying all that the local geography has to offer by hiking, biking, and running during study breaks.

During my Sophomore year at Middlebury, after learning Objective-C and Apple's iOS SDK, I started an LLC called Strabo with Will Potter and Parker Woodworth. We partnered with Green Mountain Digital, also known as NatureShare, during the summer of 2012. While working with Strabo in their Woodstock, Vermont offices, I fine-tuned my programming skills and learned a lot about both computers and people - it was a great experience and an incredible opportunity to work with the talented folks who created Yonder.

Although I am focusing back on Physics these days, I put my programming skills to good use by doing some freelance work. If you have an idea or a project that you would like to discuss, I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to get in touch by sending me an email nate.beatty@gmail.com
